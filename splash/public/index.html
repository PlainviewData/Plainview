About

We archive with context

Plainview lets you make permanent links that send your audiences to what the page looked like when you made the link, either showing the unedited original page or the HTML snapshot of the original page if any changes were made since. We achieve this by working with archive.is, the premier web archival service.

We provide tools to content creators, commentators, investigators, journalists, and the communities they serve. As our first step, we provide permanent linking to any content on the web. Next, we will allow you to automatically annotate anything on the web with the commentary you post alongside your links. Finally, we will optimize annotation and link sharing for public social media posts and news sites so that people viewing a page will see your commentary about it, no matter where you posted it.
